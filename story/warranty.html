Refund and Return Policy



iPolish, Inc. warranties its products against manufacturer defects. If for any reason you are not 100% satisfied with our products and purchased the product from www.iPolish.fashion within 30 days from your return request, just contact us and we’ll be happy to provide a FREE pre-paid return label for any order shipped within the contiguous USA (lower 48 states). Return the product to us within 30 days of when the order was shipped, along with a copy of the original receipt to receive online store credit or a complete replacement.

After 30 days of purchase, up to 90 days after purchase, we are only able to offer online store credit. Please return the product, along with the original receipt, to iPolish, Inc., PO Box XXXX, Titusville, FL 34994 USA to receive your online store credit, or replacement product. After 90 days of purchase, products are not eligible for returns or refunds. Customers from outside the contiguous United States are not eligible for returns or refunds. iPolish, Inc. does not accept returns from 3rd party distributors or retailers - please return the product to the store you purchased it at.

At this time, iPolish, Inc. does not provide cash refunds.

To obtain refills or accessories, please contact us, or shop at your favorite store that stocks iPolish® products. Thank you for your trust and support.
