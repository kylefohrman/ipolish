Profits for Purpose



Space Coast, Florida USA is often referred to as America’s second Silicon Valley. However, unlike the San Francisco Bay area, zero consumer technology-focused investment funds call Florida’s space coast home.

Recognizing that without funding, their vision of building a better tomorrow would proceed at a snail’s pace, a team of the world‘s best scientists and brightest engineers set out to solve and find a solution.

iPolish, Inc. is the first in a series of technology companies envisioned by this creative team. Designed to bring fun and joy to our loyal customers, some of the profits generated by iPolish, Inc. will be directed into the research needed to crack several of the hardest problems facing our civilization. Profits for Purpose is our philosophy. Our vision is to drive solutions from innovative technologies that can fundamentally improve the quality of life for everyone, globally.
