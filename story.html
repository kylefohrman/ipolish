Our Story



Like many companies before it, the 12-year journey to create iPolish® started amidst humble beginnings… with hope and in a garage. Built from endless testing, sweat and perseverance, iPolish® is the first consumer product from a new class of electronic devices. Futuristic ‘paint-on’ electronic devices are envisioned to disrupt the ‘electronics-in-a-box’ form used by all electronic device manufacturers, for the last 150 years. After all, what surfaces can you paint?

The engineers and scientists behind the iPolish brand are part of the US Space Industry. These best-of-the-best rocket researchers and builders are literally taking us to worlds beyond our own. Today iPolish helps you look incredible. Tomorrow, iPolish-like devices could play a huge role in exploring space. Your trust and support today helps make that happen by allowing their research to accelerate for a more amazing tomorrow. Thank you for being awesome!
